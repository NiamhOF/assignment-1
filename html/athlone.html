Project Description The works will expand the current Garda Station (c.1945) and a link constructed to the adjacent, disused Governemnt Building (c.1938). The Gardai have been using the Government Building for storage, a gym and immigration services. It is in poor condition in many parts. The new works will fit-out both buildings. The existing cellblock will be demolished and a new cellblock constructed. Works have commenced on site and are currently delayed due to the discovery of archaeology.
